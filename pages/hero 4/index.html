Dash

He is the middle child and eldest son of Bob and Helen and is the younger brother to Violet and older brother to Jack-Jack. His main superhero power is the ability to run super fast. He is also able to run on water as a consequence of this, as well as other physics-bending maneuvers such as running up buildings.

POWERS: Super Speed, Superior Reaction Time, Superhuman Stamina, Enhanced Durability
